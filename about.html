About Me

Hello! I'm Josh, an emerging and avid Interaction Designer and Illustrator. I have always interacted with the world around me through the lens of design; putting things in order and dreaming of ways to optimize and improve.

I am currently an undergraduate student at Simon Fraser University in the School of Interactive Arts & Technology (SIAT).

When I am not focused on work or school, I like to spend time listening to music, sketching, and advancing my passion of theme parks and immersive design!

Please visit my portfolio work here. If you would like to reach out, you can contact me by email at joshchin8880@gmail.com.
