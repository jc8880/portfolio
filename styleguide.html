Portfolio Style Guide

branding

The portfolio branding balances the simplicity and clarity of the user experience with organic and creative touches in-line with the freehand work of an illustrator. The intent of the design is to maintain exceptionally easy to use site navigation and add creative/personality based touches where possible.

The brand colour scheme focuses on pairing a light purple with black or dark gray, plus the background use of white.

#9987BD

#3b3b3b

The font, Urbanist also reinforces the clarity aspect of the brand, however it carries a friendlier look than a more simplistic sans serif font.

Regular: ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz 0123456789

Bold: ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz 0123456789

The brand imagery used to add personality to the site represents a digital illustrated style. It is well defined by outlines and organization, but can also contain intentional imperfections for a more hand-drawn feel. It can be compared to designs done by fashion illustrators.
